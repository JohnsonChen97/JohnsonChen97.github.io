Overview

The impact of the pandemic on businesses made a lot of owners struggle and adapt. Many local businesses still relied on in person interactions to make sales. The purpose of Digital Main Street's Future Proof program was to aid these businesses and help them create or improve their online presence.

The team I worked with consisted of a graphic designer, marketer, and copywriter in addition to my role as a UX/UI designer. I had the opportunity to help improve websites by making changes to the information architecture and adding/changing interactions such as buttons and plugins. These improvements were made by taking a look at the store's analytics and seeing where customers seem to be getting lost at. At the same time, I researched competitors in the same industry to get an idea of what else they can do separate themselves. I also spent some time going over design practices and teaching the CMS software to the clients as I wanted them to be able to make changes on their own in the future.

Overall, clients saw improvements of up to 40% increases in sales and about a 60% increase in newsletter signups, add to carts, and other conversions. New and returning customers were interacting with the website more leading to a successful outcome. The help of my team members also was important for reaching these achievements. Each member had an important role to help these businesses grow their online presence during tough times.
